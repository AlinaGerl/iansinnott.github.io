Let‚Äôs start with the quick wins:

  • Create layouts and pages directories under src

Now let‚Äôs talk about those directories:

  • src/layouts/: Is where you put wrapper components. Markup that will generally ‚Äúgo around‚Äù your individual pages. This usually means headers and footers.
  • src/pages/: This is the meat of a Gatsby site. It‚Äôs where the content goes. The rule of thumb is that you should construct your src/pages directory to build your URLs.
